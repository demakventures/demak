About Us

DEMAK VENTURES

Demak Ventures, Ghana's no. 1 online retail and wholesale store was established in May 2014 with the aim and vision to become the one-stop shop for retail and wholesale store in Ghana with implementation of quality customer care services both online and offline. Demak Ventures is the largest online retail store in Ghana. At inception we did an average delivery atleast once a week, today we do, on average, delivery in 5 days. Initially starting with 3 employees,Demak Ventures presently has a staff strength of about 80 young and entrepreneurial Ghanaians including our strong customer care service team available 6 days a week. Deliveries to all 16 Regions in Ghana is assured.
